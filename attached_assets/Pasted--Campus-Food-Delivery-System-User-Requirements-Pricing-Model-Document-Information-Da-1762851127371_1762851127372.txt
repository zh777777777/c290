# Campus Food Delivery System - User Requirements & Pricing Model

## Document Information
- **Date**: November 11, 2025
- **Version**: 1.0
- **Purpose**: User requirements and pricing specifications for campus food delivery platform

---

## User Requirements

### UR-1: Location-Based User Identification
**As a** student user,  
**I want to** share my current location on campus,  
**So that** the system can identify which block/building I am in and show me relevant nearby food options.

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- User can enable location services within the app
- System accurately identifies campus block/building
- Location updates automatically when user moves
- Privacy controls allow users to turn off location sharing

---

### UR-2: Smart Stall Recommendation System
**As a** student user,  
**I want to** select my food preferences and cravings,  
**So that** the system's algorithm can recommend the nearest stalls with the shortest queues and best match for my preferences.

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- User can input/select food preferences (cuisine type, dietary restrictions, cravings)
- Algorithm considers: proximity, queue length, food type match
- Recommendations are ranked by relevance
- Real-time queue information is displayed
- User can filter and sort recommendations

---

### UR-3: Peer-to-Peer Campus Food Delivery
**As a** student user,  
**I want to** request food delivery from other students on campus,  
**So that** I can receive my food without leaving my location (e.g., during study sessions, classes).

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- User can select delivery option when ordering
- User can specify exact delivery location on campus
- User receives real-time updates on delivery status
- User can track delivery person's location
- User can rate and review delivery experience

---

**As a** student delivery person,  
**I want to** accept food delivery requests within campus,  
**So that** I can earn money/vouchers while moving between locations on campus.

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- Delivery person can toggle availability status
- System shows available delivery requests with pickup/dropoff locations
- Delivery person can accept/reject requests
- Navigation to pickup and dropoff points is provided
- Earnings and voucher balance are tracked in profile
- Delivery person can view delivery history and statistics

---

## Pricing Model

### Context
- **Average food price range**: $4.00 - $7.00
- **Target market**: Campus students (price-sensitive)
- **Competition**: Traditional campus dining, external food delivery apps

### Delivery Fees (Paid by Customer)

| Fee Type | Amount | Conditions |
|----------|--------|------------|
| Standard delivery fee | $1.50 | All orders |
| Peak hours surcharge | +$0.50 | 12:00-14:00, 18:00-20:00 |
| **Total customer pays** | **$1.50-$2.00** | Plus food cost |

---

### Earnings for Delivery Person

#### Per-Delivery Earnings
- **Base earning**: $1.50 per delivery
- **Peak bonus**: +$0.50 during peak hours (12-2pm, 6-8pm)
- **Potential per delivery**: $1.50 - $2.00

#### Incentive Structure (Vouchers)
| Achievement | Reward |
|-------------|--------|
| 5 deliveries per week | $5 food voucher |
| 10 deliveries per week | $12 food voucher |
| Top deliverer of the month | $30 food voucher + recognition badge |

**Note**: Vouchers can be used at any participating campus food stall.

---

### Shop Owner Costs

Shop owners can choose between two pricing models:

#### Option 1: Percentage Commission
- **Commission rate**: 12% of food price
- **Cost per order**: $0.48 - $0.84 (for $4-$7 meals)
- **Best for**: Higher-priced items, premium stalls

#### Option 2: Flat Fee
- **Flat rate**: $1.00 per order
- **Best for**: Lower-priced items, high-volume stalls

#### Optional Add-ons
- **Featured listing**: $20/month (appears at top of recommendations)
- **Premium analytics**: $10/month (detailed sales insights)

## Financial Example Breakdown

### Scenario: $6.00 Meal, Standard Delivery

**Customer Payment:**
- Food cost: $6.00
- Delivery fee: $1.50
- **Total paid by customer**: **$7.50**

**Delivery Person Earnings:**
- Base delivery fee: $1.50
- Potential vouchers: Variable based on weekly deliveries
- **Direct earning**: **$1.50**

**Shop Owner Receives:**
- Food revenue: $6.00
- Platform commission (12%): -$0.72
- **Net received**: **$5.28**

**Platform Revenue:**
- Commission from shop: $0.72
- Delivery fee: $1.50
- **Total platform revenue**: **$2.22**
